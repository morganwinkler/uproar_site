Meet the CFU Team

Behind every great CrossFit Community is a team that is passionate, caring, and knowledgable. That's exactly what we have at CrossFit Uproar!

Coach Jacob

Jacob hails from the great state of Indiana, which is why he moved to Fl and the traffic has become so terrible. He has been coaching and oozing CrossFit for over a decade, and was originally introduced to the magical movements while stationed at Macdill. His goal is to slam bars, get you fit, and bring you the best hour of your day.

Coach Caitlan

Caitlan has been part of the CrossFit community for 8 years now. She is an ICU Nurse by day and wanna be athlete by night. She is extremely passionate about teaching women how to create a nourishing environment for their bodies and households. She loves coaching because it not only gives her the ability to teach others, but to to learn from others. She can't wait to work with you on your fitness and nutrition goals!

Coach Stefan

Stefan is a Mississippi native who found his way to Fl's suncoast after serving 7 years as an Army infantryman. He started CrossFit in 2008 to train for motorcycle roadracing and continued utilizing its methodology even as the racing progressed to the off-road world. Stefan pursued coaching to help others who have been frustrated by lack of fitness. In his free time he enjoys pursuising various types of fish in Tampa Bay with his kids.
